## Generative AI vs Agentic AI

## Generative AI
Generative AI (GenAI) focuses on creating content (text, images, code) based on prompts, acting as a reactive assistant.

## Agentic AI
Agentic AI is proactive, autonomously planning and executing multi-step workflows to achieve goals using tools.
AgenticAI Key uses include automating customer support, managing complex logistics, accelerating software development, and conducting autonomous, real-time data analysis for fraud detection.

- One of the biggest challenges that the enterprise facing now  is how do you move the Agentic AI applications from the POC phase to production ready business solutions?. That's the problem Bedrock Agent Core is trying to solve by providing you a set of capabilities.

* While GenAI creates, Agentic AI acts and manages processes independently. 

Key Differences:
- Behavior: GenAI is reactive (waits for a prompt). Agentic AI is proactive (initiates action to meet goals).
- Autonomy: GenAI has low autonomy; Agentic AI has high autonomy, operating with minimal supervision.
- Task Scope: GenAI handles single-step tasks. Agentic AI manages complex, multi-step workflows.
- Function: GenAI generates content based on patterns. Agentic AI plans, decides, and utilizes tools/APIs.
