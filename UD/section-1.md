## Generative AI vs Agentic AI

## Generative AI
GenerativeAI (GenAI) focuses on creating content (text, images, code) based on prompts, acting as a reactive assistant.

## Agentic AI
AgenticAI is proactive, autonomously planning and executing multi-step workflows to achieve goals using tools.
AgenticAI usecases include:
    automating customer support
    managing complex logistics
    accelerating software development
    conducting autonomous, real-time data analysis for fraud detection.

- One of the biggest challenges that the enterprise facing now  is how do you move the AgenticAI applications from the POC phase to production ready business solutions?. That's the problem Bedrock AgentCore is trying to solve by providing you a set of capabilities.

* GenAI ---> creates
* AgenticAI ---> acts and manages processes independently. 

* Key Differences:
- Behavior:   
    GenAI is reactive (waits for a prompt).   
    Agentic AI is proactive (initiates action to meet goals).
    
- Autonomy:   
    GenAI has low autonomy;    
    Agentic AI has high autonomy, ***operating with minimal supervision***.

- Task Scope:   
    GenAI handles single-step tasks.   
    Agentic AI manages complex, multi-step workflows.

- Function:   
    GenAI generates content based on patterns.   
    Agentic AI plans, decides, and utilizes tools/APIs.
